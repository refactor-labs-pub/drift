# Drift Maintainers

This document lists the maintainers of the Drift project. Maintainers
are responsible for code review, release management, security response,
and the long-term direction of the project.

## Roles

- **Lead** — Sets project direction, has final say on contested design
  decisions, manages releases.
- **Approver** — Can approve and merge pull requests across the entire
  codebase. Mentors reviewers.
- **Reviewer** — Reviews pull requests in their area of expertise. Can
  approve PRs in their declared scope (e.g. `docs`, `cli`).

## How to become a maintainer

Drift follows a meritocratic path:

1. **Contribute consistently** over several months — code, reviews, docs,
   issue triage.
2. **An existing Lead or Approver nominates you** by opening a PR that
   adds your row to this table.
3. **Other maintainers comment.** Consensus is required; objections are
   discussed in the PR.
4. **The PR merges** when consensus is reached.

The same process is used to promote a Reviewer to Approver, or an
Approver to Lead.

## How to step down

Open a PR removing your row, or email the other maintainers privately.
We will not hold it against you. Maintainers who have been inactive for
six months may be moved to an "Emeritus" section by another maintainer.
